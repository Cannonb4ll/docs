## What is Firepit CMS?

Firepit CMS is a powerful and intuitive content management system designed for modern web development.

It offers comprehensive features including product management, course categorization, user administration, and media handling capabilities.

With its clean, user-friendly dashboard, Firepit CMS makes it easy to manage websites, handle e-commerce functionality, and organize digital content.

The system includes built-in support for managing users, roles, sales tracking, and inventory control, making it an ideal solution for businesses and educational platforms looking for a flexible and scalable CMS solution.
